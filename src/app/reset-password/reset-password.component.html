lock_open Reset Password

If you forgot your password, reset it from this page. Just enter your email address and click the reset password button; you will get an email message, containing a link to be followed for resetting the password.

When you click on that link, a new temporary password will be sent to your email message: you must then login with it, and change the password as you prefer.
